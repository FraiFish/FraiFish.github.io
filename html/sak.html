Sakuzyo Fancam

Sakuzyo (born June 2, 1994) is a Japanese composer. He is a member of Diverse System. Since the spring of 2016, he has been active as a solo artist on Sakuzyo.com. He also composes music for rhythm games such as BMS, BANDAI NAMCO's 太鼓の達人, CAPCOM's crossbeats REV., lowiro's Arcaea, Rayark's Cytus and Deemo, SEGA's CHUNITHM, maimai and オンゲキ, and MARVELOUS!'s WACCA. He is skilled at composing music with a dark and heavy atmosphere. He mainly produces artcore, but also produces music in a wide range of genres from classical music to techno.
