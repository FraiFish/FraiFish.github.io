McSpicy Wings is a snack item created and made available in 1998 in McDonalds in China, the second year after the release of the McSpicy(burger). The McSpicy Wings are "Developed for Chinese consumers", and the McSpicy burger shares similar slogans such as "Specially developed signature item for 'the Chinese stomach'".



McSpicy Wings is made of both the drumette and the wingette, with "unique marinate sauce and spice" at 800-1000 Scoville Heat Units, made from powder of a Chinese pepper known as “Tian Ying Pepper"(I could not find an English article on this pepper) packed with 40000-50000 Scoville Heat Units, and fried in canola oil. The crust is made of a mix of flour and baking powder, and the texture is crisp and tender with every bite.
